About

White Summerland is an exciting event that brings together college students from all over to come and experience the ultimate EDM music festival. This event is for students who are looking to let loose and have some fun before the upcoming academic year. The event takes place in a large outdoor venue and features some of the biggest names in EDM music. The dress code for White Summerland is all-white attire, which adds to the festival's unique atmosphere. The event provides a space for attendees to dance, sing, and enjoy themselves with friends and classmates. There are multiple stages, each featuring different EDM genres, from progressive house to trance and techno. In addition to the music, White Summerland offers a variety of food and beverage vendors, including local food trucks and other popular brands. The event also has an array of interactive activities, such as photo booths, face painting, and even a silent disco. Overall, White Summerland is an event that college students should not miss. It provides a unique and exciting experience for attendees. With its all-white dress code, incredible EDM music lineup, and fun-filled activities, White Summerland is the ultimate summer event for college students.

Date: 27th May, 2023

Location: New Horizon College of Engineering, Bengaluru
